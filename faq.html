Food Safety

What food safety measures does Strata Deli take to prevent the spread of illness?

Our meal kits are prepared by a combination of both machinery and people. We follow official Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), Food Safe requirements, and are certified by the Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA) to ensure our facilities and food handling processes are in compliance with food safety standards. 

We provide ongoing training and supervision for food safety measures with our production staff to ensure all guidelines are being followed. Examples of these measures include, but are not limited to food production staff wearing appropriate attire including non-surgical masks, smocks, hairnets, gloves, and following strict hand washing procedures.

What extra precautions are you taking to ensure food is prepared in a manner that prevents the spread of COVID-19?

We employ our standard food safety precautions and food handling standards as part of our core operations to protect against any viruses or transmission of sickness.

We perform regular deep cleans of our facility, as per the strict guidelines of health officials and the highest standards of sanitization we employ at Strata Deli.

At the beginning of each shift, we review the hand washing practices as part of our Good Manufacturing Principles (GMP's), specifically around increasing the regularity of handwashing at a minimum of once per hour.

We require vigorous handwashing after using the washroom; handling raw foods; handling allergens; touching face or body; handling garbage, compost or recycling bins; transitioning between handling different ingredients; discarding any items that have come in contact with a non-food contact surface.

We have staggered shifts, breaks, and have added extra delivery days and shifts to ensure staff have a safe physical distance between other team members. Team members also have designated washrooms and breakrooms for each shift.

ALL STAFF are required to wear PPE including a mask while working and walking throughout the facility, as well as gloves, hair net, smock and other PPE in production areas.

Will my delivery be affected in the coming weeks?

We are making changes to our delivery operations to accommodate both increased demand and to facilitate social distancing within our facility. 

Saturday Deliveries: We are moving a portion of our Sunday deliveries to Saturday. This will give drivers more space when loading the bags and will help ensure we can accommodate demand on our busiest delivery day. Check your Delivery Schedule to see if your order has been moved.

Driver Protocols: You will see that drivers are wearing gloves when making deliveries. Drivers have also been supplied with commercial grade sanitizer to use on their gloves in between deliveries. Drivers will be picking up cooler bags and ice packs, but no longer accepting bags containing paper or plastic recycling at this time.

As an additional precaution, drivers are now placing all cooler bags into garbage bags once they are picked up. Due to space constraints taken up by this safeguard, we regret that not all bags may be collected. If your bags are not picked up, simply hold onto them until your next order.

What is being done to prevent the potential spread of illness via my reusable cooler bag?

As part of our regular sanitation procedures, we thoroughly clean and disinfect the bags when they return to our facility. Cleaning is done in a separate area from our ingredient processing area to avoid any cross contamination.

Our delivery drivers are being instructed to practice no-contact delivery. This means they will limit their contact with our customers by only knocking or ringing the bell, then place delivery bags in front of your door.

Will Strata Deli have any problems supplying ingredients or add-ons?

For the most part, Strata Deli’s suppliers are continuing operations, however during these unprecedented times, some ingredient changes may occur and some of our local add-on suppliers may no longer be able to supply us, or in limited quantities. Was Strata Deli affected by any recent recalls or food safety concerns?

August 1, 2020 - Our supplier recalled raw onions. Affected customers were contacted by email and all affected onions have been removed from all upcoming orders and replaced with shallots. Strata Deli will continue to closely monitor the situation, make adjustments as appropriate and communicate with our customers as the circumstances evolve and more information becomes available. See the Canadian Food Inspection Agency website for more information.

How is Strata Deli ensuring the health and safety of its employees?

We have enabled the following policies to protect our people and take precautionary measures including: 

  • All Strata Deli employees who can work from home will be working from home fr the foreseeable future to eliminate any unnecessary risks.
  • All staff and visitors entering the facility are required to receive a non-contact temperature check, answer a COVID-19 questionnaire and provide contact information.
  • Monitoring of symptoms in our staff with protocol to send them home with pay if any flu symptoms are present. If any employees are quarantined because of COVID-19 they are eligible to receive up to 14 days of paid time off to ensure they have the time needed to return to work in good health.
  • All staff receive a PPE kit including mask and gloves upon arrival to the facility, and are required to wear a non-surgical mask while working and walking throughout the facility. Staff working in production areas are required to wear PPE including a mask, gloves, hair net, smock and other PPE. Drivers are provided with PPE and hand sanitizer.
  • Staggered shifts, breaks, and have added extra delivery days and shifts to ensure staff have a safe physical distance between other team members. Employees are asked to keep a 2-meter distance from other employees working as well as on breaks. 

Recipes and Ingredients

Can I select the recipes?

Yes! We select meals based on the taste preferences you provide during sign-up, and if you want to change anything you can do so easily through your account page.

Does everything come prepped?

Yes, for most ingredients we prep them to whatever style is required for the recipe  (diced, shredded, minced etc.) 

Everything is portioned in the exact amount required for the recipe, including the spice mixes and sauces we make in-house.

 

To preserve the quality of a few ingredients which don't keep well after being prepped we send them whole (ie. tomatoes, avocados) but ensure any required prep still keeps the recipe time under 30 minutes :)

How long will the ingredients stay fresh?
Everything is shipped to arrive fresher than you might see at the supermarket. We guarantee you’ll be able to use the ingredients for at least 4 days after you receive them. If you receive seafood with your delivery we always suggest cooking that first for optimum taste!
Where do you source your ingredients?

The quality and freshness of our ingredients are incredibly important to us, so we work directly with local purveyors and farms in BC that support sustainable practices.

Where can I find my recipe cards?

Our recipe card location has recently changed. They can now be found outside of the ingredient bags.

They will be located in either the sleeve on the outside of the cooler bag or inside the cooler bag near the proteins and add-ons.

We hope you enjoy the new format! If you still cannot find your recipe cards, a digital version can be found here: Menu This Week.

What cooking equipment and ingredients do I need to have at home?

Usually all you’ll ever need to have at home is salt, pepper, and a high temperature cooking oil (we vote for grapeseed.) Occasionally, some recipes might require milk or butter. We got you covered on everything else!

For cooking equipment, a range of pots and pans are great. Spatulas, mixing and slotted spoons too. A baking sheet, mixing bowls.

Do you use Halal certified meat?

Yes, our poultry (chicken and turkey) from Rossdown Farms located in Abbotsford and our lamb provided by Intercity Packers are Halal certified by the Halal Inspection & Certification Committee of the BC Muslim Association. 

 

All other meat we use in our meal kits (beef, pork, seafood) is not Halal certified. 

Where does Strata Deli lamb come from?

Strata Deli lamb is sourced directly from the lush pastures of South-Eastern Australia. The lamb is free-range, raised without antibiotics, and free of hormone growth promotants. Our lamb is Halal certified by the Halal Inspection & Certification Committee of the BC Muslim Association.

Do you accommodate specific diets or allergies?

We work with nutritionists to accommodate a variety of dietary preferences like vegetarian, pescatarian, or if you don’t eat certain meats. You can easily personalize your menu based on your dietary preferences and change them anytime in your account settings. If you have any severe food allergies, we do not recommend Strata Deli as an option for you.

 

While we use best practices, we cannot guarantee the complete absence of food allergens. Our meals are prepared in a facility that contains allergens including tree nuts, peanuts, fish, shellfish, dairy, soy, eggs, wheat, gluten, mustard, sesame, and sulphites. Through best practices are used when preparing the meal kits, inadvertent cross-contamination may occur.


Subscription Plans

How do I skip a delivery?

You can easily skip deliveries by logging into your account and going to the ‘My Deliveries’ page. Alongside of the date of your delivery just click the ‘Skip this Week’ button and your delivery for that week will be cancelled (as long as you do it by the weekly cutoff time)

Is there a minimum time commitment?

Nope, we're very flexible with all our subscription plans - skip or cancel any time.

Can I change my meal plan or taste preferences?

Yes, you can do this easily through your account settings page. If you want to stick with the same plan and just have one less meal for whatever reason on a given week, then you can just drop one and we’ll bill the appropriate plan.

What if I forgot my password?

No worries, reset it here.

Is it better to increase servings or increase plan size?

If you find yourself increasing your number of servings every week, you can save money and effort by increasing your plan size. 

Although increasing your number of servings gives you greater flexibility week to week, increasing your plan size instead of servings will give you the best deal.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Email us at hello@stratadeli.ca before your weekly cutoff time to cancel your account. You’ll receive a confirmation email shortly after.

When is the weekly cutoff time?

The weekly cutoff time to change or cancel orders is Wednesday at 11:59PM before your next delivery. For example, if you have a delivery scheduled for Monday, you have until the Wednesday prior to modify your delivery. 

When is my credit card billed?

After the original purchase of your subscription plan, your card will be billed Thursday morning after the weekly cutoff time has passed, shortly after midnight. Any changes made to the plan prior to the cutoff will be reflected on the billed amount, and if you have skipped a delivery, your card will not be billed.

How can I save money on Strata Deli?

You can save money per serving by switching to a bigger plan. For example, a plan with 4 meals and 4 servings each will cost less per serving than a plan with 2 meals and 2 servings each.

If you need more flexibility week to week, you can always increase your servings within your plan.  However, we recommend increasing your plan size instead of increasing servings for the best deal.

To earn free meals, refer your friends to Strata Deli! Just log into your account, click on “Invite Friends”, and share your referral link with them. For every friend who signs up and orders with your referral link, you’ll get 1 free plate credited to your account. Your friend will receive 3 free plates, so it’s a win-win situation! Remember that the friends you refer have to be new customers and cannot have an existing Strata Deli account.

About Strata Deli

Why choose Strata Deli over grocery stores?

In cost comparisons we’ve done, shopping at grocery stores can be significantly more expensive simply because we send you everything in the exact portion you need it in - no more paying for a bottle of garam masala when you only need a teaspoon or tossing out a bunch of cilantro after only using a few sprigs. And, equally importantly, you don’t contribute to the massive food waste problem in our society.

On top of this we save you a whole lot of valuable time by taking care of the recipe research, sourcing the ingredients and a whole lot of prep (not to mention the cleanup prep takes after!)

How does Strata Deli work?

It’s really simple - you just create an account and tell us your taste and delivery preferences when you sign up. Then every week, we deliver you prepped and portioned ingredients for however many meals you’d like along with easy to follow recipe cards for you to cook incredible meals in under 30 minutes. You can switch your preferences and meals, skip weeks or cancel anytime.

What's included in each delivery?

Every delivery is sent out in insulated cooler bags with fresh ingredients and easy to follow recipes separately packed for each recipe you’ve ordered. Most of our ingredients, from chopped veggies to pre-measured spices and seasoning, have been prepped for you, allowing you to save time without sacrificing flavour. All of our packaging is made from recyclable, reusable and/or compostable materials.

Why choose Strata Deli over restaurants or take-out?

Nothing beats a fresh, home-cooked meal. When you open up a Strata Deli meal kit, you’re looking at exactly what’s going into your meal—no mystery ingredients! You’ll also know that our team has put a lot of time and care into sourcing high-quality ingredients for you.

Our recipes are developed by both chefs and nutritionists to ensure you’re eating a tasty and healthy meal.

You also don’t need to spend time deciding, choosing and scheduling takeout meals. By getting simple but exciting recipes and pre-portioned ingredients delivered to your door, you can easily whip up a delicious and wholesome meal. No more sitting down to cold or soggy food.

How much does Strata Deli cost?

We strive to make Strata Deli an affordable option for daily dinners, and our cost is less than what you’d typically pay for take out or even some grocery stores - especially when you factor in all the time you’d save and avoided wastage.

Our pricing is based on how many meals and servings you order weekly, and ranges from $10-$12.25 per serving. Delivery and tax is included.

Zero Waste

How are the Zero Waste Kits cleaned before re-use?

Upon return to Strata Deli, all components of the Zero Waste Kit are washed on a contactless conveyor dishwasher. We use Health Canada-recommended disinfectants to protect the health and safety of our customers and our team.


Quality assurance testing is performed frequently on washed materials, equipment and procedures to ensure the Zero Waste Kit is completely sanitized before next use.

Why did Strata Deli develop the Zero Waste Kit?

Our mission is to deliver convenient and sustainable solutions to everyday food challenges. As a B-Corporation, it’s our responsibility to design a meal kit that is not only convenient for our customers, but also positively impacts the local community and environment.

Which ingredients will remain in soft plastics?

Meat and seafood to maintain food safety.

Spice blends and some powders affected by moisture to maintain freshness.

How much does the Zero Waste Kit cost?

Meals in the Zero Waste Kit have no additional charge. We are currently not charging a deposit on the tray or the cooler bag.

How should I clean the container?

Please rinse with warm soapy water for 20-30 seconds until all food residue is removed. Alternatively, you can load into the top rack of your dishwasher.

Then place the fully-dried cups inside the Zero Waste Kit and place it back into the cooler bag with the lid on for pick-up on next delivery. If you are not ordering again next week, you can schedule a free pick-up from your delivery settings.

Will the packaging keep my ingredients fresh for as long as the previous packaging?

There is no change to our existing shelf-life commitments. It is recommended that Strata Deli meals are consumed within four days of delivery.

How do I order Zero Waste meals?

Look for the "Packed in a Zero Waste Kit" badge on recipe images in your menu.

What happens if it breaks or I lose a piece? Will I be charged for it?

At this time, we do not charge for broken or missing pieces of the Zero Waste Kit, but in future we may introduce a small fee. If a Zero Waste Kit Component is broken, please return it to us as usual.

Why is there plastic in some of the Zero Waste Kits?

While we are constantly experimenting with different packaging solutions, some ingredients will still remain in soft plastic packaging due to food safety and quality considerations. For example, raw meat and seafood must remain in their original packaging for food safety reasons.

What we’re doing with soft plastics
For soft plastics that come with your delivery, Strata Deli can recycle them through a partnership with Urban Impact Recycling, with the exception of meat and seafood packaging.

How to return soft plastics
To return soft plastics, please rinse with soapy water and ensure removal of any food residue. Then, place the rinsed soft plastics into your Zero Waste Kit or paper bag. Please do not return meat or seafood packaging and note that we cannot accept any packaging that was not originally provided by Strata Deli.

 

Which ingredients will remain in soft plastics
Meat and seafood to maintain food safety.

Spice blends and some powders affected by moisture to maintain freshness.

What should I do if the a component of the Zero Waste Kit is damaged.

If a component is damaged please return rinse and return the component with your next delivery. If the damage occurred before your meals arrived to you, please email our customer success team at hello@stratadeli.ca to let us know.

If I choose to skip, pause or cancel, will you still pick up my Zero Waste Kit so it can be reused?

Yes! Simply request a free pick-up via delivery settings in your account or clicking this link: freshprep.ca/pickups/new

Will all your meals eventually be delivered in these kits?

We will be adding more recipe options in the Zero Waste Kit each week. Recipes can be previewed up to four weeks in advance and added to orders on the menu page. Look for the badge ‘Packed in a Zero Waste Kit’ on the weekly menu at www.freshprep.ca/menu/this-week
